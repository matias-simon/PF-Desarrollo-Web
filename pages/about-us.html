About Us

Welcome to Tindog, where man's best friend can finally find their paw-fect match. We believe that dogs are not just pets, they're family. That's why we've created a space for dog owners to connect and find compatible playmates for their furry friends. Swipe right on a picture of a dog you think your pooch would love, and let the conversation with the dog's owner begin! We're on a mission to make the world a happier place for dogs and their owners, one match at a time. So why settle for just any old dog park playdate when you can have a Tindog match made in heaven?
